Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Khoya
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya & flour, shape into balls.
  2. Fry till golden. Boil sugar, water, rose water for syrup.
  3. Soak fried balls in syrup.
  4. Garnish, serve & enjoy!

Kaju Brafi

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • ghee

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews to powder.
  2. Cook cashew powder, sugar, and ghee.
  3. Spread mixture, let it cool.
  4. Cut into diamond shapes. Kaju Barfi ready to relish!
